Safaris in East, Southern and Central Africa

We’re an independent family-owned African safari company with a strong team that’s grown up,
lived, worked and travelled extensively through Africa. Our speciality is African safaris and adventure
travel along the course of the mighty Zambezi River and then well beyond into the remotest
corners of East, Central and Southern Africa.
